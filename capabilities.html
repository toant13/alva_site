Capabilities

Specialize in 5-Axis and Complex Machining

  • Engineering

    Alva engineering department includes Manufacturing, Mechanical, and Aerospace Engineers who are also experts in Design for Manufacturability and Concurrent Engineering concepts. With combined expertise and experience, our team is able to provide customers with design and development support from the ground up. We understand preliminary design process is a very significant phase in every product design. Thus we would like to get involved in this early stage in order to support our customers:

    Minimize production costs

    Improve parts quality

    Minimize engineering changes

    Shorten the time to have new products launched to market

  • Manufacturing

    Alva Manufacturing offers full CNC milling and turning capabilities from quick turn-around/short run prototypes to medium and large production. Our team has decades of experience in CNC Machining including 3, 4, and 5-Axis milling, 3D surfacing, high-speed milling, horizontal milling, and multi-axis mill/turning. Our goal is to manufacture high quality, cost-efficient parts in order to make our customers more competitive and profitable. Besides, we also offer additional services include:

    Mechanical & hardware assembly

    Metal stamping & forming

    Mirror polishing

    Bead Blasting

Our Commitments

We always strive to lead our customers to success with the following commitments:

Highest quality – We don't let parts leave our shop until we are one hundred percent sure that they meet our customers' requirements.

On-time delivery – We believe in being honest up front and only take orders that we are able to deliver in the allotted time frame

Timely communications – Our dedicated customer service team keeps customers updated with our progress and our engineering team addresses issues as soon as they arise. We will come to you before you come to us.

Continuous improvement – We believe people are the key to success in meeting our goals. We have implemented training programs so that our employees continue to learn and stay current with the latest industry best practices in manufacturing and fabrication.
